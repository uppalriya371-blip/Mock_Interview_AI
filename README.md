## 🚀 What is Mock Interview AI?

Mock Interview AI is your personal AI interviewer that helps you practice interviews without the awkward silence, judgmental looks, or scheduling headaches.

Whether you're preparing for internships, placements, or your dream job, our AI conducts realistic interviews, evaluates your answers, and provides actionable feedback to help you improve.

## ✨ Features

🎯 **Smart Question Generation**

* Technical, HR, and behavioral questions tailored to your role.

🗣️ **Talk Like It's Real**

* Answer using voice or text.

🧠 **AI Feedback Engine**

* Get insights on clarity, relevance, confidence, and communication skills.

📊 **Performance Analytics**

* Track progress and identify improvement areas.

⚡ **Instant Evaluation**

* No waiting. No ghosting. Just feedback.

🔄 **Unlimited Practice**

* Interview as many times as you want.
